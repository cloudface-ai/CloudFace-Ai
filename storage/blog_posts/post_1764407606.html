When you upload a photo to an AI platform, you should never have to wonder what happens to it next. CloudFace AI was built around one uncompromisable promise: your face stays yours. Not stored. Not shared. Not used to train models. Not analyzed beyond what you request.

In a world where AI companies quietly turn user photos into datasets, CloudFace AI takes the opposite path. We built a system designed to forget.

The Promise Behind the Line

“Your Face. Your Data. Always.” isn’t a catchy slogan—it’s our core architecture.

It means your identity isn’t a commodity, and your photos don’t become someone else’s property. It means you stay in control from the moment you upload to the moment your output is delivered.

This philosophy shaped every engineering and design decision we’ve made.

What This Commitment Looks Like in Real Life

1. Your Photos Never Sit on Servers

Every image you upload is processed inside a secure, isolated environment.

Once the task is complete, the system automatically deletes:

  • The source image
  • Temporary working files
  • Any cached output
  • AI processing data

The deletion is permanent and immediate.

2. Encryption at Every Step

To ensure absolute privacy, CloudFace AI uses:

  • TLS 1.3 for secure, encrypted communication
  • AES-256 for temporary data in memory
  • Isolated processing containers so your data never mixes with another user’s

Your uploads are protected with the same standards used in modern banking.

3. Zero Human Access

No one at CloudFace—engineers, support, admins—can access user photos.

Internal guardrails block:

  • Manual downloads
  • Access from logs
  • Viewing images directly
  • Injection attempts

Your image stays invisible and untouchable.

4. Not Used for Training—Ever

Many AI companies quietly train their models using user uploads.

CloudFace AI does not.

Your images are never:

  • Added to training datasets
  • Used for model improvement
  • Stored for later analysis
  • Sent to third parties

Your photo exists only for the task you choose.

Why We Built It This Way

AI is evolving faster than regulations. Users deserve tools that prioritize trust even when laws don’t.

We believe powerful technology doesn’t have to compromise user rights. Privacy can be baked into the architecture—not patched on top later.

This is why CloudFace AI operates with a “forget-first” approach rather than a “store-everything” culture.
